Library

"Shaping the vision of the students" The library & information Center has one of the finest collections. Situated in an easily accessible place, it covers 150 sq. ft. and occupies two sections of the references/lending section.



  • Mission Initiate, enhance and improve the library service. To support the instructional information research and curriculum needs to the staff through effective and up to date collection development. To save the time of the user by making easy reference service.


  • Collection The Library has acquired a valuable collection of more than 6027 books. The Library has a good collection of Journals i.e. current as well as back volumes. The current journal subscription is about 20 including foreign and Indian Journals along with 115 e-journals.


  • Library Details

    Total area 150 Sq. Mt.

  • Facility and Service The library can accommodate about 75 users at a time. Separate sections are provided for diploma & degree in pharmacy students.


  • Home Issue The students get 02 (Two Book) book for a period of 07 Days.


  • Internet Facility The computerized information networking system has been a powerful tool for experimental and clinical research. Library has a server and 5 nodes available in LAN. Three (3) PC's are exclusively for Internet and CDROM reference. A good collection of audiovisual aids, specialized CD's like International Pharmaceutical Abstract up to 2000 and Medline and Drug Information full text are available for the benefit of student's staff and industry.


  • Newspaper Clipping The latest new in Pharmacy field will be Xeroxed and displayed on the notice board for update.


  • Reprographic facility Reprographic machine are in library for the users benefit.
