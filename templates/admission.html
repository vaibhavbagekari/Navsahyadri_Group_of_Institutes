  • Admission Procedure:


    • Admission to the Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) Course and B Pharmacy is as per AICTE / DTE University of Pun BATU and Govt. of Maharashtra's Norms, les & Regulations. The detailed information can be Obtained from website www.dte.org.in/www.navsahyadri.edu.in

  • Eligibility:


    • First Year Admission:


    • For Engineering :-
      • 12th Science with Physics & Mathematics and compulsory subjects along with one the Chemistry or Biotechnology or Biology or Technical or Vocational subjects and obtained at least 45% marks (at least 40% marks, in case of backward class categories & Persons with Disability candidates belonging to Maharashtra State Only) in the above subjects taken together & obtained score in CET/JEE conducted by the Competent Authority.
    • For Pharmacy :-
      • PCM / PCB Obtained at least 45% marks 40% marks, in case of backward class categories and Persons with Disability candidates belonging to Maharashtra State Only) & obtained score in CET/NEET conducted by the Computhority.

    Direct Second Year:


    Passed Diploma Course in Engineering / Pharmacy with at least 45% marks (at least 40% marks in case of candidates of backward class categories & Persons with Disability candidates belonging to Maharashtra Staten Only) in appropriate branch of Engineering from an All India Council for Technical Education or Central or State Government approved Institution or its equivalent.
