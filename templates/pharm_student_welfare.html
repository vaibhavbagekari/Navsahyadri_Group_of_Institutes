Student's Welfare

The Department of Students Welfare in association with Training placement cell of Navsahyadri group of institutes looks after the protection of rights and supervises the welfare activities of the students of Constituent Units of Navsahyadri Institute of Pharmacy. They promotes and co-ordinates the different students activities for better corporate life. They try to nurture students' mental, physical, cultural growth with various activities to improve their overall personality development and to make them civilized Indian citizens to compete in the globalized world.

Various Students Welfare prominent activities undertaken are as following:

Annual Prize Distribution (Culture /Sports).
Anti-ragging Cell.

Training & Placement Cell.

Soft Skills Development Programme.

Career Guidance Scheme.

Student-related Seminar/Conferences/Workshops/Camps etc.

Youth Festivals- Avishkar.

Scholarship Department etc.
