Laboratory

Environmental Laboratory -





Lab: Computer Center

Environmental lab is utilized by the Undergraduate students for Water Supply Engineering as well as Waste Water Engineering in Sem I and Sem II respectively.
This lab is responsible for analyzing environmental and biological samples for the presence and quantities of toxic substances.




Transportation Laboratory







Lab:Transportation Laboratory

Transportation lab is utilized by the Undergraduate students for Transportation Engineering Course in Sem I and Advanced Transportation Engineering Course (Elective) in Sem II.
The objective of this lab is to determine some of the properties of coarse aggregates and bitumen.




Engineering mechanics Laboratory





Lab: Engineering mechanics Laboratory -

This laboratory gives the basic understanding of structural member under the action of loads. The students will experience the changes in behaviour of structure due to application of loads. Basics of determinate structures will be introduced through experiment.
This lab setup adequately caters to the academic requirements of UG students .




Concrete Technology Laboratory









Lab: Concrete Technology Laboratory

Concrete Technology Laboratory provides facilities to conduct all quality control tests on cement, aggregates and concrete (Fresh and hardened).
Experiments of Advances concrete technology (BE civil) for Sem - I and Concrete Technology (SE Civil) For Sem -II are performed. Utilized for Research Project of PG Students and UG students.


Survey Laboratory





Lab: Survey Laboratory

This laboratory since its inception to the Civil Department carries instruments right from Dumpy Level to advanced Total Station to cater the need of syllabus for undergraduate students from Second and Third year engineering.
The laboratory of Surveying is well equipped with all the required instruments and tools that are helpful in the overall understanding and practical knowledge of a student
