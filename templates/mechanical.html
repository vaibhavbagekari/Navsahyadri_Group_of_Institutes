About Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at Navsahyadri College of Engineering believes in producing engineers who take a product from an idea to the market place. The department has highly qualified and experienced faculty with expertise in the areas of Design, Thermal Production, Manufacturing and CAD/CAM Engineering. The department trains students through project based learning which is inculcated in the students through the team of experienced faculty. The department has defined the vision and mission and all the necessary initiatives are undertaken to facilitate and fulfill the same. Our students will have taken many internship programs. Department provides many training programs to students so that they got placed in many multinational companies.
