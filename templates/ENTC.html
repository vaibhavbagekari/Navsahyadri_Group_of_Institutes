About Department

A warm welcome from the Department of Electronics and Tele-communication Engineering at Navsahyadri Group of Institutions Faculty of Engineering, Naigaon, Pune. The Department offers a regular four-year B.E program in “Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering”. The department has excellent infrastructure and resources in the form of modern equipment's. The Department presently has highly qualified and supporting staff. The team is working hard continuously to progress the quality of engineering education provided to the students. The activities of the Department are conducted, besides teaching, in the areas of research, consulting, training, alumni and Training, and Placement. Several workshops are provided with well-trained supervisors for soft skill development. By considering the needs of industry and research organizations, the department has advanced laboratories with high-end equipment.The core values of the department help the students to develop their overall personality and make them stand confidently in the global competitive technological environment.
